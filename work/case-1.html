The brief

A new identity for the next decade.

Atlas approached us after their fifteenth anniversary feeling like the brand no longer reflected the firm. The old visual language — heavy serif logo, navy and gold palette — felt like every other asset manager.

We built a quieter, more confident system: a custom typographic logo, a single accent color, and a website that puts the work and the people front-and-center.

"The new identity gives us something we didn't know we needed — the confidence of being recognized without being loud."
JM
James Morrison
CEO, Atlas Capital
